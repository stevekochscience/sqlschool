Mode is a collaborative analytics platform that makes it easy to analyze data and share your results. This site is an extension of Mode that exists to train people to become more analytical.

Skill Building

A plain-English guide to SQL data analysis using real data and interactive examples. This tutorial is aimed at people who have spent a little bit of time with Excel or other analytics tools, but aren't familiar with SQL. It is broken into beginning, intermediate, and advanced sections.

Skill Testing

Prep for an analytical interview using questions that SQL School students actually heard in real life. The first section tests your conceptual understanding of SQL and databases; it does not require any coding. The second part will test your ability to write basic SQL queries.

Analytics Training

Once you've got a handle on SQL, the next step is to hone your analytical process. These real-life examples of problems are like on the job training before you get the job.
